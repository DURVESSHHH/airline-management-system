Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a flight?
To book a flight, visit our homepage and use the booking widget. Enter your departure and arrival cities, dates, and number of passengers, then click 'Search Flights' to view available options.
What is the baggage allowance?
Baggage allowance varies depending on your ticket type and destination. Generally, economy class allows one carry-on bag and one checked bag up to 23kg. Please check your specific flight details for accurate information.
How can I check in online?
Online check-in is available 24 hours before your flight departure. Visit our website, go to the 'Manage Booking' section, enter your booking reference and last name, then follow the prompts to complete check-in.
What is your cancellation policy?
Our cancellation policy depends on the type of ticket you've purchased. Flexible tickets can usually be cancelled for a full refund up to 24 hours before departure. Non-refundable tickets may be eligible for a partial refund of taxes and fees. Please review your ticket terms or contact our support team for specific details.
